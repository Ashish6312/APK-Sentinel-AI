GenAI Behaviour Report

Behaviour summary

This app presents itself as a banking-app update but exhibits none of the behaviour expected of one. On launch it requests SMS and contacts access, then renders a full-screen overlay mimicking a bank verification screen while a background service quietly registers a contact point at an IP address flagged as a known command-and-control node. Combined with the SMS-interception permission pair, the most likely purpose is OTP theft to facilitate account takeover on a separate, legitimate banking app.

Family: FakeBank/Generic.B (87% conf.) MITRE: T1444 Masquerading MITRE: T1517 SMS Intercept MITRE: T1430 Location/Overlay
